What is a QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of barcode that can store information such as website links, text, contact details, or WiFi credentials. QR codes are widely used in marketing, payments, and websites.

Why Use a QR Code Generator?

QR codes help users access information quickly by scanning the code with their smartphone camera. Businesses use QR codes for marketing campaigns, restaurants use them for menus, and websites use them to share links easily.

How to Generate a QR Code

1. Enter the text or URL in the input box.
2. Click the Generate button.
3. Your QR code will appear instantly.
4. You can scan it using your smartphone camera.

Benefits of Using QR Codes

QR codes are fast, easy to scan, and can store various types of information. They are commonly used in digital payments, marketing campaigns, event tickets, and product packaging.
